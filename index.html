About Me

Hey! My name is Becky and I'm a sophomore at GWU studying computer science.

I chose to pursue computer science after watching a documentary about l33t hackerz and deciding I wanted to "be a hacker". One year into my college experience, I can safely say I'm not quite at "l33t hacker" level, but I can say that I've loved every second of learning about what computer science really is.

I'm choosing to stay pursuing computer science because I feel real accomplishment after successfully writing (and mostly debugging) a program. I feel like I'm learning skills that are real world applicable, like efficient problem solving. No matter where I choose to go with my degree, I believe I'll be able to find something to do that I love doing.

Miscellaneous facts: I'm a proud Slytherin, true neutral, and a crazy cat lady.
